The Sydney Cat Massacre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The massacre was carried out on Thursday, August 17th 2017 by office worker Morris Moresly. Morris is believed to be an incredibly unstable individual who enjoys bringing torment to the family lives of others.
