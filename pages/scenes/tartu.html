“Your Tank is never dead. Your Lexi is not a killer. Your life is free because everything is free. Nothing dies. Nothing is born. We are all one and the same. A single being reinvented as everything else. I am you. You am I. Everything is everyone. A single consciousness.”

No no no no no. Too specific. What’s the reader going to get out of that? It’s not relatable at all. Our audience will feel no connect if they don’t think it’s about them! We need it to be vague.
