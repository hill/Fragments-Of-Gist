Chapter 5

The furball hissed and scratched as Morris held it at a meter's distance away from his face. Carbucketty. What a dreadful cat.

Lexi loved it of course. But retribution is retribution. Debts must be payed. All action has consequence. You leave a screw out, you must suffer the result. Just like Morris suffered.

Morris strode out the door and walked out towards the road, cat in hand.

A continuous convoy of cars drove through the roads. The perpetual sound of motor everywhere you turned in the city. There were always cars.

There’s one right now.

Morris throws the cat.

It hits the bumper falls to the grounds and it’s limp body goes under the wheel.

***

Morris hugs Lexi. What a terrible thing to have happened. Two unrelated deaths on the same day. How dreadful!

Retribution achieved. Debts paid. Everything is at equilibrium.

How absolutely wonderful.
