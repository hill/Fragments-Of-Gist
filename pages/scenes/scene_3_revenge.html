Lexi. Morris knew it. Lexi had a thing for not following instructions. For not reading the lines carefully.

Poorly interpreting the words on the IKEA instruction pamphlets. They’re not even words! They’re symbols! Does she not understand simple spatial reasoning? Morris takes a deep sigh. He looks back at Tank. He’ll have to deal with the dog later. Now there is a much more important job. Dealing with Lexi. Expressing his pain to his ignorant girlfriend.

He closes the door behind him as he heads down the fifty four stairs. Morris enters the shared garage and finds his car in lot thirty three. He unlocks the car, slumps into the front seat and turns the ignition.

The engine revs, the car lurches forward and speeds out of the garage. Pedestrians leap out of the way as the car hurdles down Carlisle Street.
